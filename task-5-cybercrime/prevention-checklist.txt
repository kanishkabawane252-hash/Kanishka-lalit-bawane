Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist
✔ General Digital Safety
Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts.
Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on payment apps and email.
Do not share OTPs, passwords, or PINs with anyone.
Avoid clicking on unfamiliar links or offers.
Verify unknown callers or messages before responding.
✔ UPI / Financial Safety
Never scan a QR code to receive money — QR codes only SEND money.
Do not approve UPI “collect requests” unless you know the person.
Double-check recipient name before confirming payments.
✔ Reporting
Report incidents at the National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
Call Helpline 1930 immediately for UPI fraud.
