Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist
✔ DO's
Use strong privacy settings on all social media accounts.
Think carefully before posting anything publicly.
Share only verified and trustworthy information.
Use respectful and professional language in comments.
Report fake accounts, cyberbullying, or suspicious messages immediately.
✘ DON'Ts
Do not share personal information like phone number, home address, or college ID openly.
Do not post when you are angry, emotional, or stressed.
Do not upload inappropriate photos or videos that harm your reputation.
Do not click unknown links, giveaway scams, or “free gift” messages.
Do not engage in online arguments, trolling, or spreading hate.
